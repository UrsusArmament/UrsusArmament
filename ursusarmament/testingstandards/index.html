STANDARDS

Our products are evaluated using environmental testing methodologies derived from MIL-STD-810H, focusing on real-world durability, reliability, and sustained performance under stress.

Framework

MIL-STD-810H Approach

MIL-STD-810H is not a single test, but a framework of environmental methods designed to replicate operational stress conditions. We apply these methods selectively based on realistic use cases rather than treating the standard as a checklist.

Environmental Methods

Temperature Extremes

High and low temperature exposure to evaluate material stability and function.

Thermal Shock

Rapid temperature transitions to test structural integrity and sealing.

Water / Rain

Simulated precipitation and exposure to evaluate ingress protection.

Dust & Sand

Fine particulate intrusion testing for harsh environments.

Vibration

Transport and operational vibration across multiple platforms.

Shock / Drop

Impact resistance from handling, drops, and sudden force.
