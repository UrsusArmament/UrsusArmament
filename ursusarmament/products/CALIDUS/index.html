Why Thermal

HEAT DOESN'T LIE.

Image intensification amplifies available light. On a moonless night under canopy, there is no light to amplify. Thermal doesn't care — it reads temperature differential and paints a picture regardless of ambient illumination.

The CALIDUS is designed for operators who can't afford a gap in their coverage: law enforcement on night operations, search and rescue in zero-vis conditions, and tactical teams where concealment from IR illuminators is a requirement.

We're building it the way we build everything — uncompromised specs and documented at the unit level.
