01
NIJ Level IIIA Ballistic Protection

Rated to NIJ Level IIIA — protection against handgun rounds up to .44 Magnum and most pistol-caliber threats. The same protection standard used by law enforcement agencies across the country.

02
High Cut Profile

High-cut geometry clears the ears for unobstructed communication headset use and hearing protection. Maintains full ballistic coverage of the crown and sides without interfering with comms equipment.

03
ARC Rail System

Bilateral ARC accessory rails provide mounting points for tactical lights, cameras, counterweights, and other rail-compatible accessories. Compatible with the widest range of aftermarket ARC-standard accessories.

04
Custom Paint Options

Beyond standard colorways, custom paint jobs are available for unit insignia, personal markings, and operational camouflage patterns. Pricing and lead time for custom work will be confirmed at launch.

05
Multiple Standard Colorways

Available in a range of standard finishes including Black, Coyote, OD Green, and Wolf Grey. All standard colorways are priced uniformly at $400 — no premium for specific colors.

06
Open Availability

Available to law enforcement, military, and civilians where permitted by law. We believe in the right of law-abiding Americans to protect themselves. Verify local regulations before ordering.

Design Philosophy

PROTECTION YOU CAN BUILD ON.

A ballistic helmet is a platform, not just protection. The ARC rail system means your helmet works with your existing kit — lights, cameras, comms mounts — without adapter plates or compromises.

The high-cut profile is a deliberate choice. Ear coverage matters less than the ability to hear your team and communicate clearly under pressure. We designed for the way operators actually work.

Custom paint is there because unit cohesion and individual identity aren't vanity — they matter in the field. We'll support both.
