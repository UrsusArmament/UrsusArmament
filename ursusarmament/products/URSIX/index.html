Hand-Selected Gen 3 Tubes

Every URSIX tube is individually tested and documented. We publish SNR, resolution, and FOM for each unit — you know exactly what you're getting before it ships.

02
Modular Objective System

Tool-free objective swap between 25mm and 35mm lenses. Same housing, different mission profiles. No need to own multiple units.

03
Dual IR Illuminator (Operator)

Switchable 850nm overt and 940nm covert illuminators built in. No external IR laser required for close-quarters use.

04
IP67 / IP68 Waterproofing

Standard and Precision configs are rated IP67. The Operator config is rated IP68 — submersible to 1.5m for 30 minutes.

05
Universal Mount Interface

Ships with Wilcox L4-compatible shoe and Picatinny weapon mount adapter. Works with all major helmet mount systems out of the box.

06
40-Hour Battery Life

A single AA battery powers up to 40 hours of continuous operation. Runs on lithium AA in cold weather without performance loss.

Why URSIX

EVERY TUBE IS DOCUMENTED

Most manufacturers ship night vision units with a tube that meets a minimum spec. We ship units with a tube whose exact SNR, resolution, and FOM are recorded at the time of manufacture — and retrievable by serial number for the life of the unit.

If your unit ever needs service, we pull that record. If you resell it, the new owner can verify exactly what they're buying. Full chain of custody, no guesswork.

That's the standard we hold ourselves to. Not because we have to — because our customers deserve it.
