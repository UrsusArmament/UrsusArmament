URSIX

Gen 2+/3 — NV Platform

The URSIX is Ursus Armament's night vision monocular, built around hand-selected, individually documented Gen 2+/Gen 3 image intensifier tubes. Each unit is serialized, tested, and recorded as a distinct system rather than a pooled specification. While units are organized into four general performance categories, every device is ultimately sold based on its exact measured performance characteristics, allowing customers to select either a tier or a specific unit. Ruggedized for all-weather operation, compatible with multiple mounting configurations, and backed by a limited lifetime warranty on all units shipped.

Why URSIX

EVERY TUBE IS DOCUMENTED

Most manufacturers ship night vision units with a tube that meets a minimum spec. We ship units with a tube whose exact SNR, resolution, and FOM are recorded at the time of manufacture — and retrievable by serial number for the life of the unit.

If your unit ever needs service, we pull that record. If you resell it, the new owner can verify exactly what they're buying. Full chain of custody, no guesswork.

That's the standard we hold ourselves to. Not because we have to — because our customers deserve it.
