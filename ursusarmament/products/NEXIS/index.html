NEXIS
Self-Healing Mesh Network

NEXIS is the human layer of the ecosystem—connecting individuals, teams, and assets through a self-healing mesh network. It maintains shared situational awareness and reliable communication as conditions change, enabling coordinated movement and informed decisions without reliance on fixed infrastructure.

↗ Integration Planned
01
Self-Healing Mesh Architecture

Nodes automatically reroute traffic when a link is lost or jammed. No single point of failure. The network degrades gracefully and reconstitutes without operator intervention.

02
AES-256 End-to-End Encryption

Every packet on the NEXIS network is encrypted at the source and decrypted only at the authorized destination. No plaintext ever transits the air.

03
Anti-Jamming — FHSS

Frequency-hopping spread spectrum transmission makes NEXIS links resistant to broadband jamming and direction-finding. Designed from the ground up for contested RF environments.

06
Integration Architecture

NEXIS functions as a defined node-based mesh, interfacing with ASI (Autonomous Systems Interface) modules to facilitate communication and coordination through the STRIX™ Fire Control Layer.

The Bigger Picture

Communication is Key

Built as a modular mesh network, NEXIS enables teams to share position, movement, and situational data in real time—supporting informed tactical maneuvering, coordinated search and rescue operations, and distributed team awareness in environments where traditional communications may be limited or unavailable.

Each node contributes to a broader operational picture. Personnel, vehicles, and fixed positions become part of a unified network that adapts to the mission—scaling from small, dismounted teams to extended, multi-element deployments across complex terrain.

NEXIS is not a weapon system. It is an enabling layer—designed to move information reliably, efficiently, and under constraint. By maintaining connectivity and shared awareness, it allows operators to make faster, more informed decisions in dynamic environments.

As part of a larger ecosystem, NEXIS integrates with systems such as R.O.T.S. through the STRIX™ Fire Control Layer, allowing networked data to inform targeting, coordination, and response without compromising its role as a communications backbone.
