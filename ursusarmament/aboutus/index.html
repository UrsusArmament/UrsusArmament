ABOUT
URSUS ARMAMENT

Ursus Armament is a veteran-owned and operated company focused on building advanced equipment for real-world application. Founded in Alaska in 2025, the company was developed with a clear objective: create dependable tools that perform under pressure and in extreme environments.

Mission

Purpose Driven

Ursus Armament exists to design and produce equipment that supports individual capability and preparedness. Our focus is on delivering reliable, high-performance gear grounded in real-world use rather than theoretical conditions.

We build the equipment we would trust ourselves, with the goal of making that same level of capability accessible to responsible citizens.

Foundation

Built on Experience

Our products are developed and evaluated by veterans and active-duty personnel, informed by practical experience rather than marketing trends. Select systems and components have been fielded in operational environments, providing direct feedback that shapes ongoing development.

Testing emphasizes durability, consistency, and performance under stress, including environmental conditions representative of both extreme climates and real-world use.

Environment

Alaska Origins

Founded in Alaska, Ursus Armament reflects the demands of harsh and unpredictable environments. Equipment is designed with temperature extremes, exposure, and sustained use in mind—conditions that quickly expose weaknesses in both materials and design.

Approach

No Excess

We avoid unnecessary complexity and marketing-driven features. Every design decision serves a purpose, prioritizing reliability, serviceability, and long-term performance.

Ursus Armament operates with a straightforward approach—focused on building dependable equipment, maintaining fair practices, and delivering real value without compromise.
